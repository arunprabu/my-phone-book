Forms 
  1. Template Driven Forms 
      HTML,
      HTML5 Validation
      Easy  
      Minimal learning curve  
      Not suitable for Complex validations (ex: email domain should be gmail.com, yahoo.com)
      Not suitable for Unit Testing 
      
  2. Reactive Forms / Data Driven Forms  (most prefered )
      HTML + TS 
      Little larger curve 
      moderate 
      perfect for complex validations 
      perfectly suitable for unit testing
